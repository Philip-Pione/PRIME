About PRIME

🎯

Our Mission

PRIME is a consortium of Pinellas County's law enforcement, fire, EMS, and 911 services working together to implement a unified, modern dispatch and records management system.

🔧

The Solution

We've selected Hexagon OnCall as our comprehensive software platform, providing cutting-edge CAD and RMS capabilities designed for modern public safety operations.

🚀

The Impact

This transformation will enhance interoperability, improve response times, and provide advanced features that support better decision-making in critical situations.

Why PRIME?

Currently, Pinellas County's public safety agencies operate on separate, aging CAD systems that limit coordination and information sharing. PRIME will unify these systems, creating seamless communication and enhanced capabilities across all 33 consortium members.

This isn't just an upgrade—it's a complete transformation that will improve officer safety, enhance citizen service, and prepare our region for future public safety challenges.
